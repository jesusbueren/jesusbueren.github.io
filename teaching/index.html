Advanced Course

This is a structural/quantitative macro course taught to 2nd year PhD students. In the first set of slides, I cover the general equilibrium life-cycle heterogeneous agent model in Huggett (1996). I use the material that Josep Pijoan-Mas taught me at CEMFI. In the second set of slides, I teach solution methods. I follow Heer and Maussner (2009) Chapters 4, 6, and 11. In the third set of slides, I introduce students to estimation using the method of moments, the generalized method of moments, and the simulated method of moments. I derive the large sample properties of the estimators. I follow Greene (2008) and Hayashi (2000).
